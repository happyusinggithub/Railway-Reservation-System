About this site

Overall description

This software compromises reserving and cancelling seats for the passengers,it contains information about trains, information about passengers,it contains the details of reservation fees, any concessions etc, it makes entries for reservation, waiting ,cancelled ticket.All the information is safely stored in the backed i.e SQL database.

Product perspective

Before the automation, the system suffered from the following drawbacks:

  • The existing system is highly manually involving a lot of paper work and calculation and therefore may be enormous.This has lead to inconsistency and inaccuracy in maintaining of data.
  • The data which is stored in paper only ,may be lost, stolen or destroyed due to natural calamities.
  • Due to manual nature it is difficult to update,delete,add or view the data.
